The SIS Token

The SIS Token, powered by blockchain technology, is at the heart of all transactions on the SeguraInsures platform. It offers a multitude of functionalities:

  • Payment and Fees: Users can pay premiums or fees with SIS Tokens, enjoying lower transaction costs and faster processing times compared to traditional payment methods.
  • Rewards and Loyalty: Holders of SIS Tokens benefit from a loyalty program that rewards customers with tokens for various engagements and transactions on the platform.
  • Staking and Governance: Token holders can stake their tokens to participate in governance decisions, influencing product features, and service offerings.
  • Claims Payouts: In certain policies, claims can be paid out in SIS Tokens, facilitating quick and seamless settlement of claims.

The tokenomics of the SIS Token are designed to ensure sustainable growth and value accrual within the ecosystem. A portion of the tokens is set aside for community initiatives, development, and ensuring liquidity.
